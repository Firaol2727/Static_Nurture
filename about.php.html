About Us

NURTURE is a Management consulting company established by conglomerates of professionals from academia and practitioners in the industry. Nurture is registered under Ethiopian commercial registration and business license to give training and consultancy services on management, economic, financial, social, and development issues.
The company’s brand name, nurture was coined with the motive of the founders’ and owners’ aspiration to unlock the unlimited human potential through training, coaching and advisory services to boost up managerial and leadership capacity of individuals and organizations in Ethiopia and Africa.
The company’s owners have taught in various higher educations in Ethiopia, working in executive positions in the various organizations and has engaged in consultancy and training activities of various not for profit , business and public organizations and think-tanks in Ethiopia.

Our Vision

Becoming a center of excellence for nurturing the natural talent of the human mind so as to enable realize the highest level of executive and leadership talent.

Our Mission

Nurture provides state of- the- art training, research and consultancy in organizational management, organizational development, development management, leadership, finance and economics, international business, entrepreneurship and business development servicesso as to enable realize the highest level of executive and leadership talent.
